# SmartCOI Logo Mockups

Created 4 logo concept variations for your COI tracking SaaS. All use your brand colors (emerald green #10B981) and are production-ready SVG files.

## Logo Concepts

### 1. Shield + Checkmark (`logo-concept-1.svg`)
- **Theme**: Trust, protection, security
- **Visual**: Shield with checkmark and AI sparkle accents
- **Best for**: Professional, insurance-focused branding
- **Tagline**: "Certificate Intelligence"

### 2. Document + AI Sparkles (`logo-concept-2.svg`)
- **Theme**: Smart document processing
- **Visual**: Document with AI sparkle indicators
- **Best for**: Emphasizing the AI extraction feature
- **Tagline**: "AI-Powered Compliance"

### 3. Modern Minimal Badge (`logo-concept-3.svg`)
- **Theme**: Clean, modern, approachable
- **Visual**: Circular badge with checkmark
- **Best for**: Contemporary SaaS feel
- **Tagline**: "Insurance Tracking"

### 4. "SC" Monogram (`logo-concept-4.svg`)
- **Theme**: Bold, memorable brand mark
- **Visual**: Letter monogram in rounded square
- **Best for**: Strong brand identity, app icons
- **Tagline**: "Compliance Made Smart"
